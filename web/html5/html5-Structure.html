HTML5 Page Structure

  • doctype
  • header - page header, section header, article header
  • nav - place menu here
  • section - content
  • article - piece of content
  • aside - content that is related but doesn't belong mainline
  • footer - page footer, section footer, article footer

See demo here

<!DOCTYPE HTML>

<html>

<head>
	<meta http-equiv="Content-Type" content="text/html; charset=UTF-8" />
	<title>Your Website</title>
</head>

<body>

<header>
	<nav>
		<ul>
			<li>Your menu</li>
		</ul>
	</nav>
</header>

<section>

	<article>
		<header>
			<h2>Article title</h2>
		</header>
		<p>Pellentesque habitant morbi tristique senectus et netus et malesuada fames ac turpis egestas.</p>
	</article>
	
</section>

<aside>
	<h2>About section</h2>
	<p>Donec eu libero sit amet quam egestas semper. Aenean ultricies mi vitae est. Mauris placerat eleifend leo.</p>
</aside>

<footer>
	<p>Copyright 2009 Your name</p>
</footer>

</body>

</html>
